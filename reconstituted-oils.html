The bitter fennel oil is obtained from F. vulgare var. vulgare which grows or is cultivated in central Europe, India, Japan, Argentina and North America. The oil is used in the manufacture of pickles, liqueurs, cough drops, licorice candy, soap and perfume. The word fennel is derived from the Roma "foeniculum", a variety of fragrant hay, the Romans used the plant for fodder.
